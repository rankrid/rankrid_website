Questions

Common questions, honest answers.

What services does Rankrid provide?
+
Rankrid provides end-to-end digital services including web development, SEO, local SEO, branding and design, social media marketing, digital marketing, content services, e-commerce solutions, business automation, SaaS development, UI/UX design, cloud and infrastructure management, and web maintenance. Everything under one trusted team.
How long does a typical website project take?
+
A standard business website typically takes 2 to 4 weeks. E-commerce projects take 4 to 8 weeks, and custom web applications can take 8 to 16 weeks depending on complexity. We provide a clear timeline and milestones at the start of every project.
How long does SEO take to show results?
+
Local SEO results are typically visible within 30 to 60 days. Broader SEO campaigns usually show measurable improvement within 90 days, with strong results at the 6-month mark. SEO is a long-term investment that compounds — the longer you invest, the stronger the returns.
Do you provide ongoing support after launch?
+
Yes. We offer AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) plans that include security updates, performance monitoring, uptime tracking, regular backups, and 24/7 support. We do not disappear after launch — we remain your long-term technology partner.
Which cities in India do you serve?
+
We work with businesses across India — Mumbai, Pune, Bengaluru, Delhi, Hyderabad, Chennai, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Surat, Nagpur, and beyond. All our services are delivered remotely, so location is never a barrier.
Can you handle our entire digital presence?
+
Absolutely. Our Pro plan is designed for exactly this — one team managing your website, SEO, social media, paid ads, automation, cloud infrastructure, and support. One point of contact, complete accountability, zero coordination overhead for you.
