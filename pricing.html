FAQs

Pricing questions answered

Are there any hidden fees? ▼
Never. Every quote includes all costs upfront. If scope changes, we discuss it with you before any additional charges apply. No surprises.
Can I cancel my monthly plan anytime? ▼
Yes — all our monthly plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel with 30 days notice. We're confident in our results so we don't need to lock you in.
Do you offer discounts for annual plans? ▼
Yes — clients who pay annually receive 2 months free (equivalent to a ~17% discount). Contact us for annual pricing on any plan.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
UPI, bank transfer, Razorpay (cards, net banking), and cheque for corporate clients. GST invoices provided for all payments.
Can I start with a one-time project and upgrade later? ▼
Absolutely. Many clients start with a one-time website or audit, see the quality of our work, and then move to a monthly plan. There's no pressure either way.
