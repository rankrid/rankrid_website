FAQs

Common questions

Which social platforms do you manage? ▼
Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), YouTube, and Pinterest. We recommend focusing on 2–3 platforms where your audience is most active rather than spreading thin.
Do you create the content or do we supply it? ▼
We create everything — graphics, captions, and reels. You just need to supply any product photos or specific assets you want us to use. We handle the rest.
How many posts per week? ▼
Our standard plan includes 4–5 posts per week per platform. We can adjust frequency based on your goals and engagement data.
Can you run ads alongside organic posts? ▼
Yes — paid ads and organic content work best together. Ads dramatically accelerate reach and results when combined with a strong organic presence.
